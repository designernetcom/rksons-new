GPV Tactical Vest

Sub Category: Tactical

Color: Blue, Black, Beige, and Olive Green

Size: Small, Medium, Large, Xlarge, XXLarge, 2XLarge, 3XLarge, 4XLarge and 5XLarge

  • The GPV Tactical Vest offers front, back, and side protection
  • Either level II or level III A multi-hit soft armor
  • Up armor with Level III or Level IV plates
  • Equipped with 4 point adjustability for easy fitment
  • 5 customizable pouches supplied as standard.
  • The inner ballistic panels are removable and the outer cover machine is washable.

On the GPV Tactical vest, the pouches and holster are fixed to the vest and the configuration is permanent. The configiration can be tailored to the customer's needs. The vest is used by Law Enforcement, Security Companies, and CPF / Neighbourhood Watch members.
