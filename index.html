Years of industry expertise

Since being founded on 21st June 1993, the company has been a pioneer in the industry for being a regular supplier of high end technology systems and solutions in prime areas of defense and security on a global scale. Over the years we have established ourselves as a brand in the Indian defense industry by being the top supplier of these equipment in terms of number of units sold and positive client feedback.

The strength of the company lies in the ability of procuring the best possible solution for the various problems being encountered by the Indian armed and Police forces and delivering it in record time to help combat them. This is possible as a result of our excellent associations with our respected technology partners who themselves are well established OEM’s in their respective fields.
